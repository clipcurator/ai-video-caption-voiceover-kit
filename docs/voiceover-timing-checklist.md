## Timing guide

| Output length | Suggested voiceover density |
|---:|---|
| 30 seconds | 65-85 English words or 90-130 Chinese characters |
| 60 seconds | 120-160 English words or 180-240 Chinese characters |
| 90 seconds | 180-240 English words or 280-380 Chinese characters |
| 3 minutes | 360-500 English words or 600-900 Chinese characters |

## Review checklist

- The opening line explains why the viewer should care.
- The script leaves room for visual moments.
- The narration does not over-explain what is obvious on screen.
- Important names and terms are easy to pronounce.
- Captions can be split cleanly by sentence or phrase.
