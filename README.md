# AI Video Caption and Voiceover Kit

AI video captions and voiceovers help turn raw edits into understandable, platform-ready short videos. This repository collects caption style guides, voiceover templates, subtitle review checklists, and narration workflow notes for AI-assisted video production.

It is designed for creators who need repeatable caption and voiceover systems across film recaps, long-video clips, ads, tutorials, and social videos.

## Caption styles

| Style | Best for | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Minimal captions | Clean story clips | Short lines, low visual noise |
| Keyword emphasis | Educational or recap content | Highlight names, numbers, or plot terms |
| High-energy captions | TikTok, Shorts, Reels | Faster rhythm, stronger emphasis |
| Bilingual captions | Global distribution | Keep both languages readable |
| Accessibility captions | Tutorials, explainers | Prioritize accuracy and clarity |

## Voiceover workflow

1. Define the audience and target platform.
2. Choose the narration role: explainer, storyteller, reviewer, host, or dramatic narrator.
3. Convert source material into a voiceover outline.
4. Write the voiceover in short spoken lines.
5. Check timing against video length.
6. Add caption emphasis and pronunciation notes.
7. Review for factual accuracy and platform fit.

## Tool notes

Captioning and voiceover work best when they are connected to the edit plan. If the system understands the source video, script, subtitles, and narration together, creators can reduce manual back-and-forth.

[Recapo.ai](https://recapo.ai/) is one relevant product to evaluate for workflows that combine long-video understanding, script generation, subtitles, voiceover, and edited video output.
